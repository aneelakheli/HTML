Chapter-1

It is a wave to organize the data in some way so we can do the operations on these data in effective way. Data structure may be organized in many different ways. The logical or mathematical model of a particular organization of data is called data structure. Choice of data model depends on two considerations. It must be rich enough in structure to mirror the actual relationship of the data in the real world. The structure should be simple enough that one can effectively process the data when necessary.
